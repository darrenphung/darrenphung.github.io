My name is Darren Phung. I’m a product design graduate from The University of Sydney and studied a Bachelor of Design Computing. I’m passionate about design and human experiences, and quite frankly this is why I aspire to be a UX designer.

I love visual design. From photography, videography, graphic design, illustration and animation, these are all things that I have tinkered with and very proficient in. This paired with my curiosity and desire to know why people do things and have certain tendencies, this lays the foundation for my journey to learn new skills and become a more well-rounded designer.

I have previously interned as a UX/UI Designer with Engenesis.
